Privacy Policy

NuZephyr Health Inc collects phone numbers solely for the purpose of account authentication and security verification. Phone numbers are used to send one-time passcodes (OTP) when users attempt to log in or verify their account.

NuZephyr Health Inc does not use phone numbers for marketing purposes and does not share personal information with third parties for advertising.

Message frequency is user-initiated and occurs only during authentication events.
